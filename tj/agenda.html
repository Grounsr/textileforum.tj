23 сентябри соли 2026 · Hyatt Regency Dushanbe · Як рӯз пур аз муҳокимаҳои стратегӣ,: ҷаласаҳои панелӣ ва имкониятҳои шабакавӣ дар соҳаи нассоҷӣ.

Бақайдгирӣ
Бақайдгирии иштирокчиён ва нетворкинг
09:00 – 09:30
Церемония официального открытия
Приветственные выступления высокого уровня
Открытие
  • Приветственное слово Премьер‑министра Республики Таджикистан.
  • Выступление Министра промышленности и новых технологий РТ.
  • Выступление Президента ITMF.
09:40 – 10:30
Пленарное заседание
“From cotton to brand: Central Asian textiles in the global economy”
Plenary
Moderator: Representative of the Ministry of Industry and New Technologies
  • Global textile industry trends: sustainability, digitalisation and circular economy.
  • The role of Tajikistan in regional value chains.
  • Strategic outlook for light industry development up to 2030.
  • Panel discussion with international experts.
10:30 – 10:50
Coffee break
10:50 – 12:00
Panel session I
“Green chain: sustainable cotton, traceability and carbon regulation”
GIZ
Organised by: GIZ
  • Sustainable cotton production: Better Cotton, organic certification and their implementation in the region.
  • Traceability: Digital Product Passport and blockchain solutions for textiles.
  • CBAM: EU carbon border adjustment mechanism and implications for exporters.
  • Regenerative agriculture and water‑saving technologies.
  • Circular economy and waste recycling in textile production.
12:00 – 13:00
Networking lunch
13:00 – 14:30
Panel session II
“Digital spinning mill: automation, AI and Industry 4.0”
Industry 4.0
Participants: LMW, Uster Technologies, Bajaj, Rieter
  • Concept of a fully automated spinning mill: implementation experience and results (LMW).
  • Data‑driven quality management and AI‑based online monitoring (Uster Technologies).
  • Modern ginning technologies preserving fibre quality (Bajaj).
  • Spinning from recycled and blended fibres: energy efficiency and cost reduction (Rieter).
Format: moderator (10 min) + 4×10 min case presentations + 10 min Q&A.
14:30 – 14:50
Coffee break & technology exhibition
14:50 – 16:10
Panel session III
“Investing in modernisation: green finance & export support”
ADB
Organised by: Asian Development Bank (ADB)
  • International financial instruments for textile modernisation.
  • Green loans and grants: eligibility criteria and application process.
  • The role of development banks and IFIs in textile projects.
  • Export finance, factoring and export risk insurance.
  • Public‑private partnerships in the textile sector.
16:10 – 17:00
Panel session IV
“Skills of the future: dual education and digital competences”
Education
Institutions: universities from Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Tashkent Textile Institute
  • Competences for a modern textile factory: from machine operator to process engineer.
  • Dual education models and partnerships between industry and universities.
  • Upskilling and reskilling programmes for the textile sector.
  • Attracting youth and promoting women’s leadership in textiles.
17:00 – 18:45
Closing session
“Made in Tajikistan: branding and entering global markets”
Closing
  • What international buyers are looking for: sustainability, transparency and quality.
  • Positioning Tajik textile products on global markets.
  • Using B2B platforms, marketplaces and digital marketing.
  • Collaboration with designers and international brands.
18:45 – 19:00
Summary of the Forum and closing remarks
