About the forum

International Textile Forum
Tajikistan 2026

A strategic investment platform providing direct access to full‑cycle textile projects in Tajikistan, operating predominantly on the basis of 98% renewable hydropower.

Executive summary

From cotton to brand on green energy

International Textile Forum Tajikistan–2026 is a specialized marketplace for investors seeking full‑cycle textile projects: from cotton fibre to finished products, powered by 98% renewable hydropower and backed by long‑term tax and customs incentives, investment agreements with parliamentary ratification and strong institutional support.

  • Over 100,000 tons of cotton fibre annually with potential for higher value‑added exports.
  • 98% renewable electricity and preferential tariffs (up to 50% discount) for full‑cycle textile enterprises.
  • Portfolio of prepared feasibility studies for textile clusters and modernisation projects.
  • Integration into global supply chains through Industry 4.0 technologies and digital traceability.
Venue

The Forum will take place at Hyatt Regency Dushanbe, a leading international hotel in the capital of Tajikistan, offering modern conference facilities for high‑level events.
