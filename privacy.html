Privacy Policy

This page explains how the International Textile Forum Tajikistan 2026 website collects and uses personal data provided by visitors and registered participants.

1. Data controller

The International Textile Forum website is operated by the Ministry of Industry and New Technologies of the Republic of Tajikistan (“we”, “us”, “our”).

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identification and contact details (name, surname, email, phone number, organization, country).
  • Information about your participation (type of participation, fields of interest).
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, basic analytics cookies).

3. How we collect data

We collect data when you:

  • submit registration and contact forms on the website;
  • contact us by email or phone using the details published on the website;
  • browse the website, where basic usage data and cookies may be processed by our hosting provider.

4. Purposes of processing

We process personal data in order to:

  • register and manage your participation in the Forum and related events;
  • communicate with you regarding programme updates, logistics and organizational details;
  • respond to your questions and requests sent via contact forms or email;
  • prepare anonymised statistics to improve the Forum and its website.

5. Legal basis

We process personal data based on your consent (when you submit forms) and our legitimate interest in organizing and promoting the Forum and related events.

6. Data sharing

We may share your personal data with:

  • service providers involved in the organization of the Forum (event management, IT support, venue);
  • international partners and co‑organisers of the Forum, where necessary for participation and logistics;
  • public authorities if required by applicable law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as it is necessary for the purposes described above or as required by applicable law, after which the data will be deleted or anonymised.

8. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to request access to your personal data, ask for correction or deletion, or object to certain types of processing.

9. Cookies and analytics

Our hosting provider may use basic technical cookies and server logs to ensure proper functioning and security of the website. We do not use cookies for profiling or targeted advertising.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact: info@textileforum.tj.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date of the latest update will be indicated at the top of this page.
